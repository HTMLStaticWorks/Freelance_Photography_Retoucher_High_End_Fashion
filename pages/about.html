Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Milan, RetouchStudio began as an exclusive boutique serving a handful of premier fashion photographers. Today, we are a globally recognized post-production house.

We believe that a retoucher is the silent partner to the photographer. Our job isn't to change the narrative, but to eliminate distractions and let the true essence of the subject shine through.
