Service Overview

High-End Beauty

Flawless, non-destructive skin retouching for macro beauty and cosmetics campaigns.

The Micro-Dodging Technique

Unlike basic blurring techniques that destroy skin texture, we utilize pixel-level micro-dodging and burning. This method involves manually lightening shadows and darkening highlights on a microscopic level to smooth out blemishes while retaining 100% of the natural pore structure.

The result is skin that looks flawless yet undeniably real, meeting the rigorous standards of international beauty and cosmetics brands.
